How to make puff-puff

Article by Favour salako published on may 15, 2022.

Puff-Puff is a very popular West African street food that is quick and easy to make with different variations. Dangerously delicious and satisfying. Most West African countries are familiar with the popular street food Puff-Puff, which is known by different names depending on the country. Ghanaians call it Bofrot (togbei), Cameroonians and Nigerians call it Puff-Puff (Puff) or beignet in French.
The basic ingredient consist of: flour, sugar, yeast, water, salt and oil for deep frying. The yeast is to be put in a bowl and mixed with a bit of warm water- room temperature, sugar and salt is then added as desired and the mixture is stirred. Add flourand keep stirring till mixture is smooth and pasty. cover with damp towel and leave to rise for 1-2 hours. The oil should be heated for 10 mins medium temp, a lot of oil is needed as it is deep frying. Then one just has to scoop the dough in balls with a spoon or hand into the oil, wait 3 mins then flip after that sieve Puff-puff out of oil and onto a saviette.
One can fry it a bit longer depending on how crunchy or brown one wants the Puff-puff. It is an easy recipee that produces a really satisfying result. Puff-Puff can be eaten as breakfast, desert or even as a snack. A taste of Puff-Puff using this recipee will captivate ones palate and leave you wanting more as it has done millions of West Africans and Nigerians till date.
